# Alinma Bank Retail Products (Shariah-Compliant)

Disclaimer: rates and fees below are illustrative examples published on alinma.com (pricing update of February 2026). Actual offers are subject to Alinma Bank's credit policies. This is general information, not financial advice.

Alinma Bank is a Saudi bank regulated by the Saudi Central Bank, and all its products are approved by its Shariah Committee.

## Savings accounts (Mudaraba-based)
Alinma savings accounts pay a monthly, Shariah-compliant expected profit with unlimited withdrawals and no lock-in, which makes them well suited for emergency funds and seasonal sinking funds.
- Daily balance package: 1.50% AER (SAR 1 – 1,000,000), 1.75% AER (1M – 3M), 2.20% AER (above 3M).
- Minimum balance package: 2.00% AER (SAR 1 – 1,000,000), 2.22% AER (1M – 3M), 2.70% AER (above 3M).
Best fit: emergency fund, Ramadan/school/travel sinking funds, and short-term savings toward a goal — the money stays liquid while earning a halal return.

## Auto financing (Murabaha)
Published illustrative examples for the Stock–Auto product:
- 36,711 SAR financed over 4 years at 3.00% APR ≈ 800 SAR per month.
- 40,758 SAR financed over 5 years at 4.00% APR ≈ 900 SAR per month.
- 45,972 SAR financed over 5 years at 3.61% APR ≈ 1,000 SAR per month.
Administrative fee for non-real-estate financing: 0.5% of the financing amount or 2,500 SAR, whichever is less.
Best fit: buying a car without draining savings below the emergency fund target. Compare total murabaha cost against paying cash; financing at ~3–4% APR can be reasonable when it protects liquidity.

## Real estate financing (Murabaha)
- Directly via Alinma: example of 500,000 SAR over 25 years at 5.49% fixed APR ≈ 2,996 SAR per month.
- With Ministry of Housing / REDF support: example of 500,000 SAR over 25 years at 4.41% fixed APR ≈ 2,703 SAR per month.
Administrative fee: 1% of the financing amount or 5,000 SAR, whichever is less. Early repayment costs the remaining amount plus profit for the next three months.
Best fit: home purchase goals; eligible customers should check REDF-supported programs first, since the subsidized rate is meaningfully lower.

## Credit cards — use with caution
- Annual fees: Alinma Platinum 100 SAR, Signature 250 SAR, Infinite 300 SAR. mada debit cards are free to issue and renew.
- The revolving monthly profit rate is 2.5%, which corresponds to an APR of roughly 38–40% or more depending on the card.
- Paying only the minimum (5% or 100 SAR) on a fully used limit can take 88 to 114 months to settle the balance.
Rule of thumb: a credit card is a payment tool, not a financing tool. Never carry a revolving balance; for any real financing need, murabaha financing at 3–6% APR is far cheaper than credit card debt at ~38%+ APR.

## Everyday banking
Current accounts are free to open, local mada purchases are free, cash withdrawals and deposits at Alinma ATMs are free, and bills can be paid through SADAD at no charge. Local transfers cost between 0.575 and 8.05 SAR electronically depending on the amount.

## Product matching guide
- Emergency fund or seasonal sinking fund → savings account (minimum balance package for the higher expected rate).
- Car purchase goal → compare cash purchase vs. auto murabaha (~3–4% APR); financing is worth considering when a cash purchase would break the emergency fund.
- Home purchase goal → REDF-supported murabaha first if eligible, then direct murabaha.
- Short-term seasonal costs (Ramadan, Eid, school, travel) → pre-saved sinking fund in a savings account; never credit card debt.
- Zakat planning → savings account statements make it easy to compute the zakat base at the hawl.
